Biography

30+ Years of Hands-on Engineering

Michael Williams is a London-based engineer, technologist, author and researcher with over 30 years of experience spanning hardware R&D, embedded systems, full-stack web development and AI integration. He is the founder of BIS Publishing (est. 2009) and the Black Scientists & Inventors Platform — a comprehensive Django-based educational platform celebrating Black excellence in STEM.

His academic foundations — a BEng in Electrical & Electronic Engineering and an MSc in Sensors & Intelligent Instrumentation — give him rare end-to-end depth: from writing firmware for microcontrollers to building multi-agent AI systems deployed in production. He is available for employment or consultancy targeting AI Engineering, Full-Stack Web Development, IoT/Embedded Systems and Robotics roles.

As an author, Michael has published multiple books on Black scientists and inventors, including titles in the Black Women Scientists & Inventors series and Africa's Journey to Industry 4.0. He is passionate about STEM representation and has been building platforms for STEM education since 2009.
