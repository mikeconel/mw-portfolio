Biography

Engineer, Entrepreneur & AI Systems Builder

Michael Williams is a London-based engineer and technologist with a BEng in Electrical & Electronic Engineering and an MSc in Sensors & Intelligent Instrumentation. His career spans embedded systems and sensors R&D, 20 years founding and leading an international publishing business, and a deliberate return to engineering — now focused on AI integration, full-stack web development, and IoT/edge computing.

Over the past 12 months, Michael designed and deployed a production-grade AI-powered Django platform (blackscientistsandinventors.com) incorporating a Claude-powered chatbot, multi-agent automation systems, LMS with AI-guided GCSE courses, Stripe/PayPal payments, and Celery pipelines. He also built a FastAPI + MQTT + Streamlit real-time IoT pipeline now serving as a BSI course capstone project, and developed computer vision systems using OpenCV and Mediapipe.

He combines deep technical capability with commercial acumen and a track record of building things that ship and generate results — including contributing to £250,000 in National Lottery funding secured by the Windrush Foundation through a digital platform he built.
